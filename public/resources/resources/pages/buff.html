High-Speed Buffing

Does my vehicle need to buffed and compounded?

A vehicle that is less than 4 years old, or a garage-kept vehicle that has received an annual paint sealant treatment, will likely not need buffing.

If you're not sure if your vehicle needs buffing, contact us and one of our highly-trained detailers will provide assistance in determining what's best for your vehicle.

Why is buffing necessary?

Through the years, the harsh elements in the environment can take it's toll on your vehicle's finish. Things that affect your vehicle's finish include: UV radiation, airborne contaminants, hard-water, contaminants in the rain, tree-sap, oxidation, etc.

What's involved in this service?

  1. Rubbing compound applied to the finish using a high-speed rotary buffer.
  2. Polish the finish using a high-speed rotary buffer.
  3. A coat of paint sealant applied for final protection.
