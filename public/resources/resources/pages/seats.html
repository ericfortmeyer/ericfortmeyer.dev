Upholstered Seat Cleaning

What's Involved?

If you choose this service as an add-on to the Basic Detail, your upholstered seats will be cleaned. Pioneer Cleaning uses a dry-cleaning method to clean upholstered seats in vehicles. An extractor or steam cleaner will not be used on the seats. Seats are often dry within 2 hours!

*add $40 to your service

Since damp weather may affect drying time, please schedule this service during dry weather. Some stains and pet hair will not be removed.



*price may be more depending on the size and condition of the vehicle
